# 🗳️ Building the Future of Voter Education with AI

I am thrilled to share my latest project: **Sistemaa Electoral**, an AI-powered Election Education Assistant built for the **Prompt Wars Challenge 2**!

🗳️ **The Problem:** 
For many, especially first-time voters, the election process can feel overwhelming, complex, and full of jargon. This often leads to confusion and hesitation in participating in the democratic process.

🚀 **The Solution:**
Sistemaa Electoral is designed to be a friendly, non-biased guide. It breaks down complex electoral concepts into simple, actionable steps using a "Logic-First" approach.

✨ **Key Highlights:**
- **Guided Learning Path**: A structured 5-step journey from Basics to Election Day Actions.
- **Adaptive Personalization**: The assistant changes its tone and explanation depth based on whether you're a beginner or an aware voter.
- **Confusion Recovery**: Using micro-chunking and analogies, it detects when you're stuck and simplifies the conversation automatically.
- **Robust Logic Engine**: Built with a modular service architecture for high reliability.

🛠️ **Tech Stack:**
- Node.js & Express
- Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS (for that premium, lightweight feel!)
- Google Cloud Run (Containerized with Docker)
- Antigravity AI Engine

Building this has been an incredible journey in exploring how AI can simplify complex governance processes for everyday citizens.
